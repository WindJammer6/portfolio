Projects

UROP - Human in the loop learning algorithms for Intelligence In Smart Cities

Collaborated with other SUTD students Aditya Kumar and Lee Wai Shun with the mentorship of Dr Marie Siew under the Undergraduate Research Opportunities Programme (UROP) to publish an accepted paper at the Artificial Intelligence for Wireless Communications and Networking (AI4WCN) Workshop in 2024, held by the Association for the Advancement of Artificial Intelligence (AAAI), titled 'Towards Adversarially Robust Human-in-the-Loop Learning for HVAC Systems', with the introduction of a novel framework, RARL_HIL and testing it in unique a Heating, Ventilation and Air-Conditioning (HVAC) system simulation.

UROP - Telegram chatbot for novice programmers to learn coding

With the mentorship of Professor Oka Kurniawan , I created a functional prototype of a telegram chatbot, with the integration of Retrieval Augmented Generation, Prompt Engineering and Chatbase custom GPT LLM to help smoothen that starting step for new programmers into the programming world. I also created a Streamlit website that allows course instructors to manage the telegram chatbot and view student conversations with the telegram chatbot as learning review.
